PHNOM PENH

Royal University of Phnom Penh

        - Establishment: Founded in 1960 as the National Pedagogical Institute, later becoming RUPP in 1996.
        - Location: Located in Phnom Penh, Cambodia's capital city.
        - Academics: RUPP offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various fields.
        - Faculty: RUPP boasts a highly qualified faculty.

Kirirom Institute Of Technology (KIT)
